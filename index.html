BTS - Bangtan Sonyeondan

BTS (Korean: 방탄소년단; RR: Bangtan Sonyeondan), also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a seven-member South Korean boy band formed in Seoul in 2013.The group have staged several world tours.The group initially formed as teenagers under Big Hit Entertainment.

Album

  • Map of The Soul: Persona
  • Love Yourself: Answer
  • Love Yourself: Tear
  • You Never Walk Alone
  • Love Yourself: Her

EXO

Exo (Korean: 엑소; stylized in all caps) is a South Korean–Chinese boy band based in Seoul, with nine members: Xiumin, Suho, Lay, Baekhyun, Chen, Chanyeol, D.O., Kai and Sehun.The band was formed by SM Entertainment in 2011 and debuted in 2012. Exo releases and performs music in Korean, Mandarin, and Japanese.

Album

  • Don't Mess Up My Tempo
  • The War
  • For Life
  • Universe
  • Sing for You

Pentagon

Pentagon (Korean: 펜타곤; stylized as PENTAGON; abbreviated as PTG) is a South Korean multinational boy band formed by Cube Entertainment in 2016.[1] The group consists of nine members: Jinho, Hui, Hongseok, Shinwon, Yeo One, Yan An, Yuto, Kino and Wooseok.

  • Ceremony
